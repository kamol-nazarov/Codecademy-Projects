Kale Caesar Quinoa Salad with Roasted Chicken

Kale and quinoa provide a healthy base for roasted chicken topped with a light caesar sauce

Total Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of Kale
  • 1 cup of Quinoa
  • 2 tbsp of Olive Oil
  • 1 Chicken Breast
  • Caesar Dressing

Preparations

  1. Prepare quinoa and roast chicken until golden brown and 165 degrees in the middle.

    Time: 40 minutes

  2. Toss quinoa, chicken, kale, and Caesar dressing until coated thoroughly.

    Time: 4 minutes

  3. Add walnuts and olive oil as a garnish

    Time: 1 minute
